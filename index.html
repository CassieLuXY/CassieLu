Hi, I'm Lu Xingye.

I am currently a research assistant professor at the Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). Before joining PolyU, I was a post-doctoral fellow at the Department of Computer Science, the University of Hong Kong. I received my Ph.D. degree from PolyU in 2020 under the supervision of Prof. Man Ho Allen Au. I received my B.Eng degree from the Southeast University (Nanjing, China) in 2014. During 2013-2014, I was an exchange student at KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Stockholm, Sweden).

I am interested in theoretical cryptography and its applications, especially in:

  • Post-Quantum Cryptography

  • Zero-Knowledge Proofs

  • Blockchain Technology
